Manzini is the vibrant heart of Eswatini, serving as the country's main commercial center. Known for its bustling central market, diverse shopping districts, and rich cultural heritage, the city attracts visitors and traders from across the region. The city's strategic location in the middle of the country makes it an important transportation hub.
