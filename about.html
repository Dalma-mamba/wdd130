    About Dalnjebo Mamba Rafting

    Dalnjebo Mamba Rafting is a premier rafting outfitter offering guided river adventures for families, thrill-seekers, and nature lovers. With experienced guides, modern safety gear, and a commitment to sustainable practices, we create memorable trips on rivers of all difficulties.

    • Family & Beginner Trips: Calm stretches, scenic floats, and patient guides—perfect for first-timers and kids.
    • Intermediate Runs: Exciting rapids and dynamic rapids sections with expert guide instruction and close support.
    • Advanced & Expedition Trips: Remote multi-day routes, technical whitewater, backcountry camping, and guided wilderness experiences.
    • Specialty Offerings: Private charters, corporate team-building, photography tours, and custom itineraries.

    Every trip includes safety briefings, certified guides, life jackets, and optional gear rental. We tailor trips by skill level and group size and provide flexible booking, multi-day packages, and local lodging recommendations.

    History

    Founded in 1998 by lifelong river guide Juma Dalnjebo, Dalnjebo Mamba Rafting started as a community-run outfit offering short, safe floats on the nearby river. What began as a neighborhood pastime quickly grew into a regional business as word spread about the team's local knowledge, friendly guides, and commitment to safety.

    Throughout the 2000s the company expanded its offerings from family-friendly day trips to technical runs and multi-day expeditions. Dalnjebo Mamba invested heavily in guide training and modern safety equipment, earning certifications and partnerships with regional conservation groups. The company became known for combining adventurous whitewater with responsible stewardship of river ecosystems.

    Today Dalnjebo Mamba is a full-service rafting outfitter that emphasizes sustainable tourism, community employment, and authentic outdoor experiences. They continue to support local conservation projects, mentor young guides, and design trips that welcome beginners while challenging experienced rafters.

    • 1998 — Company founded by Juma Dalnjebo.
    • 2006 — First certified guide school and expanded trip roster.
    • 2014 — Launched the River Care Fund for habitat restoration.
    • 2022 — Added remote multi-day expeditions and partner lodges.
